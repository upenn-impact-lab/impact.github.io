The IMPACT lab is dedicated to developing CT and cone-beam CT imaging technologies guided by a rigorous understanding of imaging science. Active research areas include: (1) development and analysis of deep learning image formation algorithms, (2) spectral imaging: system design and material decomposition algorithms, (3) radiomcis standardization guided by imaging physics, (4) non-circular trajectory for cone-beam CT: design, reconstruction, and clinical translation
