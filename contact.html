Open Positions

We always welcome highly motivated and highly skilled students, post-doctoral researchers, and research scientists to join the group. For prospective students, strong programming and quantitative skills are necessary. Knowledge in signal processing, random processes, and optimization are most relevant to projects in the lab. For post-doctoral fellows and research scientists, expertise in at least one of the following areas is required: (1) imaging physics/imaging chain modeling, 2) subjective image quality evaluation, 3) analytic and/or model-based tomographic reconstruction, 4) hardware integration. Please contact us with a copy of your CV and specific research areas you are interested in.
